## 📌 Project Overview

This project analyzes the **annual sales and order data** of **Vrinda Garment Store** — an Indian e-commerce fashion retailer selling across platforms like Amazon, Flipkart, Myntra, Meesho, Ajio, and Nalli.

The goal was to clean raw sales data, build pivot-based analysis, and design a **fully interactive Excel Dashboard** to uncover business insights that can help improve sales strategy.

---

## 🎯 Objective

> Analyze Vrinda Store's 2023 sales data to understand customer behavior and identify the most effective channels, regions, and demographics — and recommend a targeted growth strategy.

---

```
Garment_Store_Report.xlsx
│
├── Raw Data          → Original transactional sales data

└── Dashboard         → Interactive visual dashboard with slicers
```

## 🔍 Analysis Performed

| # | Analysis | Description |
|---|----------|-------------|
| 1 | **Orders vs Sales** | Monthly trend of total revenue and order count |
| 2 | **Order Status** | Breakdown of Delivered, Cancelled, Returned, Refunded |
| 3 | **Sales: Men vs Women** | Gender-wise purchase distribution |
| 4 | **Top 5 States** | Highest revenue-generating states |
| 5 | **Age vs Gender** | Which age group shops more, by gender |
| 6 | **Channel Performance** | Order share across all selling platforms |

---

## 💡 Key Insights

- 👩 **Women** account for ~**65%** of all purchases — significantly more than men
- 🗺️ **Maharashtra, Karnataka & Uttar Pradesh** are the top 3 states, contributing ~**35%** of total sales
- 👨‍👩‍👧 The **Adult age group (30–49 yrs)** is the highest contributing segment (~**50%** of orders)
- 🛒 **Amazon, Flipkart & Myntra** together drive ~**80%** of all orders

---

## ✅ Final Recommendation

> **Target women customers aged 30–49 years** living in **Maharashtra, Karnataka, and Uttar Pradesh** by showing targeted **ads, offers, and coupons** on **Amazon, Flipkart, and Myntra**.

This data-backed strategy focuses resources on the highest-converting customer segment and channels.
